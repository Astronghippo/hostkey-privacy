HostKey is designed with privacy first. We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Your WiFi credentials never leave your device.

What Data We Collect

None. HostKey operates 100% offline and does not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics.

  • No account or sign-up required
  • No data sent to our servers (we don't have any)
  • No third-party analytics or tracking
  • No advertising or ad networks

Data Stored on Your Device

HostKey stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • WiFi network details - Network names (SSIDs), passwords, and labels you enter
  • App preferences - Your settings like theme preference
  • Purchase status - Whether you've upgraded to Pro (verified through Google Play)

This data is stored in your device's local storage and is never transmitted anywhere. If you uninstall the app, this data is deleted.

Location Permission (Optional)

HostKey offers an optional "Auto-Detect WiFi" feature that can read your current WiFi network name. To use this feature, Android requires location permission.

Important:

  • This feature is completely optional
  • Your location is never stored, tracked, or transmitted
  • The permission is only used to read the WiFi network name
  • You can always enter network names manually instead

In-App Purchases

HostKey offers a Pro upgrade through Google Play. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google Play and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

HostKey does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for use by people of all ages.

Data Security

Since all data stays on your device and is never transmitted, your WiFi credentials are as secure as your device itself. We recommend using your device's built-in security features (screen lock, encryption) to protect your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Since we don't collect your contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes.
